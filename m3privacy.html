Privacy Policy Overview

It is Derek Perry’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information Derek Perry may collect while operating the game, Minimal Match 3. This Privacy Policy applies to Minimal Match 3 (hereinafter, “the game”, “Derek Perry's game”, or “Minimal Match 3”). Derek Perry respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personally identifiable information you may provide Derek Perry through the game. Derek Perry has adopted this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) to explain what information may be collected in the game, how Derek Perry uses this information, and under what circumstances Derek Perry may disclose the information to third parties. This Privacy Policy applies only to information Derek Perry collects through the game and does not apply to the collection of information from other sources.

This Privacy Policy sets forth the general rules and policies governing your experience with the game. Depending on your activities when playing the game, you may be required to agree to additional terms and conditions.

Game User Data

Like most game developers, Derek Perry collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that devices and servers typically make available, such as the device/device type, language preference, and the date and time of each user request. Derek Perry’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Derek Perry’s game users play/use the game. From time to time, Derek Perry may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of the game.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain users of Derek Perry’s games choose to interact with Derek Perry in ways that require Derek Perry to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Derek Perry gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. Derek Perry runs analytics to understand how you interact with the game which should continuously improve your gaming experience (e.g. identifying that a feature is too difficult for most users).

Security

The security of your Personal Information is important to Derek Perry, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While Derek Perry strives to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, Derek Perry cannot guarantee absolute security.

Personal Data Concerning Children

Derek Perry does not knowingly collect any personal data concerning children under 16 years old. In the unlikely event that Derek Perry has (or you believe Derek Perry has) collected information from a child, please contact Derek Perry by using the contact info at the bottom of this privacy policy. Derek Perry will take all necessary steps to purge the data from relevant systems.

Links To External Sites

This game may contain links to external sites that are not operated by Derek Perry. If you click on a third party link, you will be directed to that third party’s site. Derek Perry strongly advises you to review the Privacy Policy and terms and conditions of every site you visit.

Derek Perry has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites, products or services.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Derek Perry discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Derek Perry’s behalf or to provide services available at Derek Perry’s game, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using Derek Perry’s game, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. Derek Perry will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, Derek Perry discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only in response to a subpoena, court order or other governmental request, or when Derek Perry believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of Derek Perry, third parties or the public at large.

If you send Derek Perry a request (for example via a support email or via one of the feedback mechanisms), Derek Perry reserves the right to store it privately in order to help clarify or respond to your request. Derek Perry takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Aggregated Statistics

Derek Perry may collect statistics about the behavior of users in the game. Derek Perry may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Derek Perry does not disclose your personally-identifying information.

Business Transfers

If Derek Perry, or substantially all of his assets, were acquired, or in the unlikely event that Derek Perry goes out of business or enters bankruptcy, user information would be one of the assets that is transferred or acquired by a third party. You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Derek Perry may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
